Christian Sewing (German pronunciation: ['krɪsti̯an 'ze:vɪŋ]; born 24 April 1970) is a German banker, and the CEO of Deutsche Bank. He has been a member of the management board since 1 January 2015. On 8 April 2018 he was appointed chief executive officer (CEO) of Deutsche Bank.

Early life[edit]

Christian Sewing was born on 24 April 1970. He earned his Abitur in 1989. He completed an apprenticeship programme at Deutsche Bank in Bielefeld, graduating with a banking qualification from the Chamber of Industry and Commerce in 1991. He then studied business administration at the Frankfurt School of Finance & Management, in Bielefeld and Hamburg.

Career[edit]

Sewing first worked in the branches of Deutsche Bank in Bielefeld and Hamburg, then as a junior corporate client advisor in Toronto, as Chief Credit Officer in Japan, and six years as a risk manager in London. From 2005 until 2007, Sewing was a member of the management board of Deutsche Genossenschafts-Hypothekenbank [de].

Sewing was head of group audit from June 2013 to February 2015, prior to which he held a number of management positions in risk management. From 2012 to 2013, he was deputy chief risk officer. From 2010 to 2012, he was chief credit officer. He has worked in Frankfurt, London, Singapore, Tokyo and Toronto. From January 2016, Sewing oversaw Deutsche Bank's private and commercial banking division, which includes the lender's network of German retail branches, but has a low profile outside of Germany.[1] He earned 2.9 million euros for 2017.[2]

On 8 April 2018, Sewing became CEO at Deutsche Bank, replacing John Cryan.[3] That year, he led Deutsche Bank to its first profit in four years and launched talks with Commerzbank on a potential merger.[4] After his first year, his 7 million euro compensation made him one of the best paid chief executives in European banking.[5] As of September, Christian will spend 15% of his monthly net salary buying the German lender’s shares as part of his efforts to revive the bank's profitability. [6]

Other activities[edit]

Corporate boards[edit]

  • Deutsche Postbank, Member of the Supervisory Board (since 2012)

Non-profit organizations[edit]

  • Federation of German Industries (BDI), Member of the Presidium (2017-2018)[7]
  • Association of German Banks (BdB), Member of the Board of Directors[8]
  • Working Group of Protestant Businesses (AEU), Member of the Board of Trustees[9]

Personal life[edit]

Sewing is a passionate tennis player.[10] He is married with four children.[11]
