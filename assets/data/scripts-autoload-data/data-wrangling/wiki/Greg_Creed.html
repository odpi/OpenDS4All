Greg Creed

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Greg Creed (born c.1958) is the CEO of Yum! Brands[1] , and has been since January 2015. He was the CEO of Taco Bell from February 2011 until that time.[2] He began as a Taco Bell executive in 2001, and began his career as a Yum! executive in 1994. Prior to that, he worked at Unilever. He holds a business degree from the Queensland University of Technology.[3] He serves on the board of directors of the Whirlpool Corporation.[4]
