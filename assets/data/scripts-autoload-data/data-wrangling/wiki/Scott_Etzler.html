Scott Etzler was formerly President of InterCall.[1] He was named President in 1998. InterCall, a subsidiary of West Corporation, is a conferencing and collaboration service provider. Etzler holds a bachelor of science degree in Business Economics from Indiana University Bloomington and a master of business administration degree from Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University[citation needed].

Etzler serves on the Indiana University Kelley School of Business Dean's Advisory Council[2] Northwestern’s Kellogg School of Management Advisory Board, and on the Board of Steppenwolf Theatre[3] in Chicago. He is also a member of the Economic Club, Big Shoulders Foundation and the Executives Club of Chicago.[4]
