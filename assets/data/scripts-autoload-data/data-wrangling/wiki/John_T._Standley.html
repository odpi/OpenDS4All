John T. Standley
Born(1964-04-20)April 20, 1964

John T. Standley (April 20, 1964) is an American businessman.[1]

Standley is the former chairman and CEO of Rite Aid Corporation. He was replaced as CEO on August 08, 2019 by Heyward Donigan. [2][3]
